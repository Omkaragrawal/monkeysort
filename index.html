MonkeySort

This is an experiment in human-driven sorting.

Reason: it's hard for a human to sort many things from most favorable to least favorable. At best you can implement a SelectionSort algorithm by picking your most favorite thing of all, then the second favorite, et cetera.

This method is not effective and prone to error.

MonkeySort implements an algorithm with minimal number of comparisons, and you only have to pick a favorite from two things.

Pick one: don't care
